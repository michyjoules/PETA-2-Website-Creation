Far Eastern University

Far Eastern University (FEU) is one of the leading private universities in the Philippines, known for its commitment to accessible and quality education. Founded by Dr. Nicanor Reyes, it strives to equip students with a strong academic foundation and real-world skills. FEU is dedicated to providing students with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in a constantly evolving world. The university fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, creativity, and responsible digital citizenship among its students.

FEU offers a wide range of degree programs designed to provide students with a strong foundation in their chosen fields. These programs prepare them for professional success and leadership in various industries.

STEM-Related Organizations

Below are some organizations related to STEM field. These organizations will not only nurture your leadership role, but also form a family-found friendship.

FEU HS STEM Society (STEM Soc)

STEM Soc is an academic organization at FEU High School for STEM students. It helps develop skills, excellence, and character while preparing future innovators, scientists, engineers, and architects to lead and contribute in STEM fields.

Be part of the pi!

Eureka Science Organization (ESO)

ESO is a group for students interested in science, especially Physics and Chemistry. It creates programs and projects to solve science-related issues. ESO helps students develop their skills and use their knowledge to make a difference, following its motto: Imagine, Invent, Innovate.

Imagine, Invent, and Innovate with ESO!

Health Allied Association (HAA)

The HAA is a student organization at FEU High School under the Student Activities Office (SAO). It helps students develop skills and knowledge in medical and health sciences, preparing them for future careers in the field. The organization provides training and experiences to build a strong foundation for those aspiring students who want to pursue medicine or health-related courses.

Learn the skillful way of HAA!

Brilliant, Young, and Technology-Empowered Students (BYTES)

BYTES is a student-led organization at FEU High School focused on technology and innovation. It helps Junior and Senior High School students develop essential tech skills for success in ICT and everyday life, following its Constitution and By-Laws.

Achieve your tech-y self with BYTES!
